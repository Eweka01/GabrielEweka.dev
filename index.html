About Me

I'm a passionate DevOps Engineer with hands-on experience in designing, automating, and maintaining scalable cloud infrastructure across AWS and hybrid environments. My work centers around creating efficient, secure, and resilient systems through automation, CI/CD, and Infrastructure as Code (IaC).

I’ve delivered several AWS-based projects, including containerized deployments, CI/CD pipelines, real-time analytics pipelines, and baseline security configurations using tools like Terraform, Ansible, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD, and Prometheus/Grafana. My approach always includes security at every stage—implementing IAM best practices, CIS benchmarks, and compliance frameworks such as NIST 800-53 and ISO 27001 to ensure system integrity and governance.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity Operations Management, which strengthen my ability to merge DevOps efficiency with security-driven design. I’m also the creator of the Blueprint Case Study Series, where I break down real DevOps and Cloud projects into clear, story-driven technical guides to help others learn practical, hands-on engineering.
